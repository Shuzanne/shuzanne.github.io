About Me


Hello, I'm Suzanne Reilly the person behind Sooz Draws.

I like to tell stories and create things that connect with people.

You'll find out when you talk to me that I'm from Scotland. I'm pretty passionate about it. I studied traditional 2D Animation and gained experience in freelance game art before making a leap down to London.

Working in marketing design gave me an opportunity to create things for big brands to use on Facebook and Instagram. I also got to lead a small but awesome team of talented designers, which I loved.

I've worked with clients like Volkswagen, Worldpay and Macmillan Cancer Support and love collaborating with individual developers and creatives.

If you want to collaborate or want to hear more about my work, say hello!
