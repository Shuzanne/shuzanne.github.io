About Me


Hello, I'm Suzanne "Sooz" Reilly.

I like to tell stories and make cool things that connect with people.

I'm originally from Scotland where I studied traditional 2D Animation. From there I gained experience in freelance game art before taking the leap down to London in 2013. I then worked in marketing design for some time creating for Facebook and Instagram campaigns and led a small but awesome team of designers.

I've worked with clients such as Worldpay and Tiger Games, as well as collaborations with individual developers and creatives.

I love to learn and meet new people, come say hello for work or collaboration enquiries!
