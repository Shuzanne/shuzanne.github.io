About Me


Hi, my name is Sooz (she/her).

I’m a director, illustrator and animator, living in Glasgow (UK) with my partner and crazy wee dog Lily.

I’m a lover of things hand-made, playful and human.

I’m interested in how animation can help explain tricky concepts or emotional subjects in a more sensitive, accessible way.

Topics I love: gender, bodies, human experience, grass-roots activism, feminism, nature, science.

If you'd like to collaborate or discuss your next project with me, please get in touch :)
